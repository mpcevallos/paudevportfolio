### Project overwiew:

I contributed to the development and launch of VIU, a cross-platform mobile application available for Android and iOS. My responsibilities included designing the app logo and icon, creating user interface screens tailored for both operating systems, and producing promotional materials such as posters to support the marketing efforts.

## Role:

UX/UI Designer & Brand Designer

## Key Contributions:

- Designed a distinctive and scalable logo and app icon.
- Created UI/UX screens adapted to both iOS and Android platforms, ensuring a consistent and user-friendly experience.
- Collaborated closely with the development team to ensure design accuracy and alignment with user needs.
- Designed a promotional poster to support the app’s launch and visibility.

### Tools

- Adobe Illustrator
- Adobe Photoshop
- HTML5
- CSS3

---

- Developed a mobile application that facilitated easier payment processing for clients, allowing them to manage their account status in real-time.
- Implemented features that enabled users to access special offers and promotions, enhancing customer engagement and retention.
- Designed an intuitive user interface using Adobe Illustrator, which included custom icons, buttons, and layouts to improve the overall user experience.
- Collaborated closely with the development team to ensure the accurate implementation of designs, resulting in a user-friendly and functional app.

### Impact

The app successfully launched on Android and iOS, supported by a strong visual identity and clear user experience.

- Improved payment efficiency and transparency for clients, leading to increased customer satisfaction and streamlined account management.
