## Project Overview

This is an academic project aimed at developing a platform that enables patients to create an account with a username and password, access a control panel for managing medical appointments with their doctors, browse the medical center's available specialties, and update their personal information.

![Metabolic Medical Center Web App for Appointment Scheduling](../../../public/assets/images/projects/mmc-webapp.png)

## Role

- UX/UI Designer
- Frontend Development

## 🚀 Project Features

- A platform functioning as an administrator for a medical center.
- Allows patients to schedule appointments through the platform.
- Provides access to the catalog of medical specialties directly on the platform.
- Enables users to create an account and edit their contact information.
